King’s Metropolitan Worship Embassy is a Christ-Centered Church Community dedicated to Authentic Worship, Biblical Teaching, and City-Focused Mission. Founded in (2023) under the leadership of (DOMINO IGAH ALICK) and registered with CAC in (2025), the Embassy began as a small gathering of believers hungry for God’s presence and has grown into a vibrant, multi-generational congregation in Kaduna South Local Government Area of Kaduna State.

At the heart of King’s Metropolitan Worship Embassy. is the vision to raise Kingdom ambassadors who impact culture with excellence, compassion, and integrity. Through dynamic worship services, discipleship pathways, and practical outreach initiatives, the Embassy equips believers to live out their faith in families, workplaces, campuses, and communities. Ministries for children, youth, young adults, and families ensure that every generation is nurtured and empowered for Kingdom purpose.

Beyond the church walls, King’s Metropolitan Worship Embassy. is committed to city transformation, and partnering with schools, Non-Government Organisations, and community leaders to serve through benevolence, skill development, and missions. With a growing digital presence and a heart for global outreach, the Embassy continues to expand its influence, carrying the message of Jesus Christ to nations.
