About Us

King’s Metropolitan Worship Embassy is a dynamic, faith-driven community where lives are transformed through the power of the Word, worship, and fellowship. Our church is founded on the mission to glorify God, build strong disciples, and impact society with kingdom principles.

We believe in developing every believer to live as a true “ambassador of the King,” bringing light, love, and leadership into every sphere of life. Our church focuses on teaching sound doctrine, fostering a culture of compassion, and empowering individuals to serve their communities.

Under the guidance of dedicated leadership, King’s Metropolitan Worship Embassy continues to expand its outreach through evangelism, social impact programs, and mentoring for youth and families across Nigeria.
